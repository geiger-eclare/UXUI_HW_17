Skills

User-Based Research

I possess a strong skill set in user-based research, encompassing the ability to conduct insightful user interviews and systematically gather data on the behaviors and preferences of individuals within specific organizational contexts. With a keen aptitude for organizing and analyzing user data, I effectively integrate fundamental principles derived from research sources into my work, allowing me to draw informed conclusions that drive user-centric decision-making, ultimately enhancing the overall user experience and product design.

Information Infrastructure

My skillset in information architecture is centered on translating user and research data into well-crafted design plans. By conceptualizing the layout of information and features within websites, apps, games, and more, I ensure that users can effortlessly navigate and interact with the digital environment. With a strong grasp of information structuring, I facilitate seamless user experiences by strategically organizing content, resulting in intuitive and user-friendly designs that meet both user needs and business objectives.

Design Iteration and Protoyping

My skills are well-honed in design iteration and prototyping. I excel in the creation of comprehensive wireframes for a variety of digital interfaces and the subsequent transformation of these wireframes into interactive prototypes. Whether it's low-fidelity drafts or high-fidelity designs, I ensure that every aspect of the user experience is carefully considered. This skillset also includes comprehensive user testing of these prototypes, afterwhich I am adept at pinpointing and enacting specific design changes in order to alleviate any remaining burden on the user and stakeholding organization.
